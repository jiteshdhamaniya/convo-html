Introducing CanReseal®

An Innovation from Canovation LLC

Metal Packaging with a Difference

CanReseal® is the Future of Cans

The Canovation CanReseal® end is the technological development that finally enables cans to be hermetically resealed.

The elegantly simple solution to this age old problem in canning was to create a deeper end with threads. This elementary change to the humble can end now allows a cap to be screwed in to reseal the can.

CanReseal’s® unique design may be adapted to any packaging type that has an end seamed onto it. From carbonated to still beverages, dry to wet foods, to consumer goods and pharmaceuticals, the CanReseal® provides an advantageous, resealable packaging option.

The Canovation CanReseal® the is ecologically sustainable, easily integrated and manufacturable, highly marketable and differentiating, and economically viable Can of the Future!

CanReseal® Technology

The CanReseal® technology is based on Canovation’s patented, socket end design with internal threading.
This is the key change needed to convert any existing can end to a resealable one.

Development has had a principal focus on proving the viability of the important features needed for Canovation’s solution to economic and effective can resealability. Initially designed as a 202 size, carbonated beverage end, the CanReseal® uses existing forming methods to draw a deeper, socketed shell.

This socket is threaded to create a screw-in fastener, the second fastener ever developed that is made from the same material as the can end. The first one was the now ubiquitous rivet, back in 1959.

Current CanReseal® ends will seam onto standard cans, perform at retort pressures, and is compatible with existing manufacturing and filling processes.

The advent of the patented CanReseal® threaded socket end technology brings resealability to cans and the metal packaging industry into the 21st century.

The CanReseal® offers the promise of resealability on all can types, and Canovation has recently begun efforts to deliver a cost competitive and effective, resealable food end. Consumer convenience and value are the driving forces behind the development of the CanReseal® food end. While mobility is a factor for resealing a beverage, the need for resealing food is strictly for storage. This type of usage allows for food cans to be sold only with the ability to reseal. Food brands can sell CanReseal® compatible caps that are reusable, dish-washer safe, and offer whole new marketing and service opportunities.

The CanReseal® end makes cans resealable, but it also makes them interactive. The addition of threading on a regular can gives it abilities normally associated with other packaging types. With input from industry partners and further development, the Canovation CanReseal® could be custom tailored to provide significant additional value across a number of packaging types, requirements and industries. Canovation LLC - The CanReseal Company

An Innovation from Canovation LLC

Canovation LLC is a Florida based company that was founded in 2012 to develop after a clever solution for resealable cans was suggested to a group of entreprenurial investors. The concept was novel in ways that were not innitially imagined, and prototype development successfully created the revolutionary CanReseal® socketed can end.

Several resealablecan technologies were known to be available, but all were very complex designs or just a metal bottles of one sort or another. The elegantly simple way that the CanReseal® achieves resealability speaks volumes to its future success and influence in the metal packaging industry.

The CanReseal® technology is proving itself to become the foundation to many innovations in canning over the next few years, and Canovation looks to remain a leader in this field.
